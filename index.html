HACK TO SCHOOL

The University of Texas at Dallas

Welcome back to school

Join us for a twelve hour hackathon organized by the HackUTD team!

Exclusive to UTD students, this hackathon is, you guessed it, an education based hackathon where you are able to create software or mobile apps that could help students with productivity, academics, or stress relief!

Frequently Asked Questions

When is Hack to School?

It will on Saturday, April 18th. The event will start at 9am to 9pm!

Where is Hack to School?

It will be located on the first floor of ECSW!

Who can attend?

Any UTD Student that is over 18 years old, all majors are welcomed!

Do I need to apply?

We encourage you to apply as we can not guarantee walk-ins for this event.

What should I bring?

UTD ID, Laptop, charger, headphones, and anything else you may need. We provide food, drinks, and hardware!

How much does it cost?

HackUTD provides food, drinks, and attendance all for free!

What projects can I make?

We encourage projects to be focused on innovating education, from academics to stress relief.
